Long-Form Pieces

These are some of the longer works I produced in my time at UM. These pieces were more in-depth, and required much more production and post-production to be completed.

This piece was done surrounding the birth of Miami's newest MLS Franchise, InterMiami. We highlighted the birth of the idea and the origins of proffesional soccer in Miami.

This piece was a profile on a once high-touted basketball player out of Orlando, Florida. His journey, however, encountered many speed bumps along the way, dealing with injuries and the loss of interest from colleges at the next level.
